Our vision

The 44 TechMakers association was created in 2020 with one goal: making the 44's people life more dynamic and full of learning, that’s why we organize different events (Conferences, workshops, hackathons…..) Under several themes. To make it short, we are here to support students during their academic journey by putting new technologies to their benefit and allowing them to exchange their knowledge and experiences. As well as helping them better project themselves in the professional world with good skills.
